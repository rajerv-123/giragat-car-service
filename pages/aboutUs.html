About Us

At Garagat, we’re revolutionizing car care with a service that’s as mobile as you are. Whether you’re at home, at work, or on the go, our app connects you to a network of trusted automotive professionals who provide everything your car needs—all with the tap of a button. We understand that life can get busy, and taking time out for car maintenance shouldn’t be a hassle. That’s why we’ve designed Garagat to bring convenience and expert care straight to you. From routine oil changes to deep cleaning and detailing, we offer a full range of services that are fast, affordable, and reliable. Our goal is to ensure that your car gets the attention it deserves, with no stress or extra time wasted. We take pride in providing high-quality, professional service, treating every car with the utmost care, just as if it were our own. With Garagat, car maintenance has never been easier. Simply choose the service you need, and let us handle the rest. Your car’s health is our priority, and we’re here to make keeping it in top condition a breeze
