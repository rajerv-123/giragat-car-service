PRIVACY POLICY

At Garagat, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using Garagat, you agree to the practices described in this policy.


1. Information We Collect

  • For Car Owners/Users:
    1. Full name
    2. Email and phone number
    3. Vehicle details (make, model, year, license plate)
    4. Location data (for finding nearby service providers)
    5. Payment information
  • For Service Providers:
    1. Business name and contact information
    2. Service location and availability
    3. List of services offered and pricing
    4. Professional credentials or licenses (if required)
  • Automatically Collected Data:
    1. Device type and operating system
    2. IP address
    3. Usage data and interactions within the App
    4. Error logs and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

Garagat uses your data to:

  • Create and manage user and provider accounts
  • Connect users with relevant service providers
  • Facilitate booking, payment, and service tracking
  • Send important updates, promotions, or support messages
  • Improve the App's performance, features, and security
  • Comply with legal or regulatory obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information:

  • With matched service providers or customers, for coordination
  • With third-party partners (e.g., payment gateways, cloud providers)
  • With authorities when required by law or to protect our users
  • In the case of business mergers, sales, or asset transfers

4. Data Protection

We use encryption, secure servers, and other industry-standard practices to protect your data. While we do our best, no system can be 100% secure, so we advise caution when sharing sensitive information.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • View or update your personal data
  • With third-party partners (e.g., payment gateways, cloud providers)
  • Request data deletion or portability
  • Withdraw consent or opt out of communications
  • Control location tracking via your device settings

6. Children's Privacy

Garagat is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Third-Party Links

The App may link to third-party sites or tools. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this policy as our services evolve. We will notify you of major changes through the App or via email. Continued use of Garagat indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
