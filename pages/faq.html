FAQ’s

  1. How do I pay for services?

    Payments are processed securely through the app using your preferred payment method. You’ll be notified of the service cost before confirming your booking, and you’ll be charged once the service is completed.

  2. Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

    Yes! You can cancel or reschedule your appointment directly through the app. Please note that cancellations may be subject to a fee depending on the timing.

  3. How do I know the service provider is trustworthy?

    All professionals in the Garagat network are thoroughly vetted and have been carefully selected based on their experience and qualifications. We only partner with trusted service providers to ensure high-quality and reliable service.

  4. What if I have an issue with the service?

    If you’re not satisfied with the service provided, please contact us immediately through the app. We’ll work with you to address any concerns and ensure the issue is resolved.
